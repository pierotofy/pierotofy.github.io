PokerMuck screenshots

Main Interface

Note the box next to "Calls". Whenever a hand from a player becomes known at showdown, PokerMuck analyzes the actions done by the player on every street and rates the type of hand that a player called, raised, checked or bet with. In the screenshot above, given the action so far, the player called preflop 33% of the time and given the cards he has shown, he called only with weak hands (in the top 50% of starting hands).
